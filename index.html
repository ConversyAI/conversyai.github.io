Frequently asked questions

How do I deploy on my domain?

Use GitHub Pages or Vercel for this static site. For your chatbot, we provision a unique URL and let you add a CNAME to use your domain.

Do I need a database?

For the static marketing site, no. For Conversy bots, we store your data securely and expose read/write endpoints.

Can I embed the bot on my website?

Yes. Copy the embed snippet from the dashboard and paste it into your site. It’s a lightweight iframe/web component.
